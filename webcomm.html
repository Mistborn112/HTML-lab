My Favourite Books

  1. The Lane Winslow murder mystery series by Iona Whishaw

    This series single handedly sparked my love for reading so it holds a special place in my heart

  2. As Long As The Lemon Trees Grow by Zoulfa Katouh

    This book was unbelievably sad and heartwrenching but it was so beautiful and i will never get over it.

  3. The Poppy War Trilogy by R.F. Kuang

    this trilogy is all about war, genocide, torture and drug abuse and the author really doesn't sugar coat any of it which makes these books kind of hard to read but they were written so well and are definitely some of the best books i've ever read.

  4. The Gentlemen Bastards Trilogy by Scott Lynch

    5. these books are dark and funny and the characters are great

  5. The Farseer Trilogy by Robin Hobb

    6. These books are all about assassins, magic and a boatload of political intrigue and the author put so much detail and care into every word that it made me want stop reading so it never ends. i stared at the wall for like 5 minutes after i finished the last book because it was so great.
